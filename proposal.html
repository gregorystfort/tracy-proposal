A three-phase build.

Foundation covers Phases 1 and 2 (brand and content). Growth adds Phase 3 (newsletter and the paid lead engine).

01
Phase 1 · Both plansBrand & Presence
Brand direction and visual identity. Logo refinement if needed, color and typography system, template library. Full optimization of Instagram and Facebook. 90-day content calendar.
02
Phase 2 · Both plansContent & Consistency
